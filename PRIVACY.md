# Privacy Policy for Real Weather

Last updated: July 2026

Real Weather ("the app") shows weather forecasts by averaging data from
several public weather models based on your device's location.

## Information we collect

- **Location.** The app requests your device's location (while in use)
  to fetch a weather forecast for where you are. Your location is sent
  to Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com), a third-party weather data provider,
  solely to request forecast data for those coordinates.
- We do not collect, store, or transmit your location anywhere else.
  We have no servers of our own — the app talks directly to Open-Meteo.
- We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or any
  personal information beyond the location used for the forecast
  request itself.

## Data retention

Your location is used only for the current forecast request and is
not stored by us. See Open-Meteo's own privacy policy for how they
handle request data: https://open-meteo.com/en/terms
